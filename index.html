Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our tire service

How long does the service take?

Standard tire changes take 30-45 minutes for both regular and immediate service appointments.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, debit cards, and e-transfer. Payment is due upon completion of service.

What if the weather is bad?

We work in most weather conditions. For extreme weather (heavy snow, ice storms), we'll reschedule for your safety and ours.

Do I need to be present during the service?

Yes, we require someone to be present for security and to ensure access to your vehicle and tires.

What if my tires don't fit properly?

Our technicians will inspect your tires before installation. If there are any issues, we'll advise you on the best course of action.

Do you provide tire storage?

Currently, we focus on tire installation services. We recommend local tire shops for storage solutions.
